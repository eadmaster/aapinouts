
9 pin D-SUB male connector (at the game console)

	  _____________
	  \ 5 4 3 2 1 /
	   \ 9 8 7 6 /
	    `~~~~~~~' 


	Pin	Name (Select=GND)	Name (Select=+5V)
	1	Up	Up
	2	Down	Down
	3	Gnd	Left
	4	Gnd	Right
	5	+5VDC	+5VDC
	6	Button A	Button B
	7	Select	Select
	8	Ground	Ground
	9	Start	Button C
