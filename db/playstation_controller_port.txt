 
9 pin Sony Playstation proprietary connector (at the controller):

	 _______________________
	| 1 2 3 | 4 5 6 | 7 8 9 |
	 \______|_______|______/


	1	DAT	Data
	2	CMD	Command
	3	N/C	Vibrations (7.6V official, 9V unofficial)
	4	GND	Ground
	5	VCC	Power (3.3V official, 5V unofficial)
	6	ATT	Attention
	7	CLK	Clock (~7kHz)
	8	N/C	Unknown
	9	ACK	Acknowledge
