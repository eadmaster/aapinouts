
Mini-DIN6 female port (on the computer):


       └┘
    6o ┌┐ o5
       ││
  4o   └┘   o3
   ─┐      ┌─
    │o    o│
     2    1

 
	1   data
	2   n/c (reserved)
	3   gnd
	4   +5v
	5   clock
	6   n/c (reserved)
