

9 pin D-SUB female connector (at the cable)

	  _____________
	  \ 5 4 3 2 1 /
	   \ 9 8 7 6 /
	    `~~~~~~~' 

    
	1	DCD	<--	Data Carrier Detect
	2	RXD	<--	Receive Data
	3	TXD	-->	Transmit Data
	4	DTR	-->	Data Terminal Ready
	5	0V/COM	---	0V or System Ground
	6	DSR	<--	Data Set Ready
	7	RTS	-->	Request to Send
	8	CTS	<--	Clear to Send
	9	RI	<--	Ring Indicator
