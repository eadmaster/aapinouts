
7 pin Serial ATA motherboard internal connector (at the host device):

	+------------------+
	|  1 2 3 4 5 6 7 | |
	| ---------------+ |
	+------------------+


	1	GND	Ground
	2	A+	Transmit+
	3	A-	Transmit-
	4	GND	Ground
	5	B-	Receive-
	6	B+	Receive+
	7	GND	Ground
